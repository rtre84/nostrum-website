About Us

Team Nostrum focused on flooding as a way to study and mitigate it’s most detrimental effects on earth and the global society. Comparisons of flood data and loss from satellite observations paired with past records, help to show us where improvements to our global infrastructure, water management techniques and storage resources should be made or improved upon. Our research shows the documented stats from historical observations. The benefit of quantifying this research and isolating it’s access gives Emergency Management Resources, First Responders, Government Agencies, The United Nations, and all other aid providers key resources to mitigate and manage a flood disaster more efficiently.

Try Out Our Simulation Today!

The app helps simulate flooding impact on various terrains. For example, you can model a city like terrain in the demo to measure impact on the terrain. It won't look exactly like a building but it will simulate impact. Our goal is to aggregate data and simulate various scenarios where you better understand the impact on any given region on earth based on available data down to the individual districts.
